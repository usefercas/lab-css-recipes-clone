Apple Pie

This was my grandmother's apple pie recipe.I have never seen another one quite like it.it will always be my favorite and has won me several first place prizes in local competitions .I hope it becomes one of your favorites as well!

Ingredients
  • 1 recipe pastry for 9 inch double crust pie
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablesoppns all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cuped packed brown sugar
  • Granny smith apples

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Melt the butter in a saucepan. Stir in four flour paste. Add water, white sugar and brow sugar and bring to a boil. Reduce temperature and let stimmer
  2. Place the buttom crust in your pan. Fill with apples, mounded slightly. Cover with a lattice work crust. Gently pour sugar and butter liquid over the crust. Pour slowly so that it dos not run off
  3. Boke 15 minutes in the preheated oven. Reduce the temperature to 350 degrees(175F). Continue baking 35 to 45 minutes until apples are soft
